Each building you pass adds to your score. Try to beat your personal best and climb the leaderboard!

Pro Tips

  • Time your swings just after passing a building for maximum momentum
  • Watch for patterns in the building layouts to anticipate gaps
  • Short, controlled swings are often better than big jumps
  • For higher scores, maintain a steady rhythm rather than erratic movements
  • Practice makes perfect - the more you play, the better you'll get!

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Spiderman Web Swing

Is this game free to play?

Yes, Spiderman Web Swing is completely free to play. There are no in-app purchases or hidden costs.

What devices can I play on?

You can play on any device with a web browser - desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones are all supported.

Is this an official Spider-Man game?

No, this is a fan-made game inspired by Spider-Man. It is not affiliated with Marvel, Sony, or any official Spider-Man franchise.

Why isn't the sound working?

Make sure your device isn't muted and that you've clicked the sound toggle button in the game. Some browsers may also require a user interaction before allowing sound to play.

Are my high scores saved?

Yes, your high scores are saved locally on your device. They will persist between gaming sessions as long as you use the same browser and don't clear your browser data.

ABOUT THE CREATOR

The story behind Spiderman Web Swing and the passion for web-slinging adventure!

The Web Developer

As a passionate fan of Spider-Man and web development, I created this game to combine my love for both worlds. Spiderman Web Swing is inspired by the classic Flappy Bird gameplay but with a superhero twist!

My goal was to create a simple yet addictive game that captures the thrill of web-slinging through New York's skyline. The game is constantly evolving with new features and improvements.

Thank you for playing Spiderman Web Swing! I hope you enjoy playing it as much as I enjoyed creating it. Feel free to share your feedback and suggestions for future updates!
